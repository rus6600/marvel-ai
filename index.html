Transition to Electronic Software Distribution

Starting late 2024, Microsoft 365 Business Standard will be widely available via Electronic Software Distribution (ESD).

ESD enables the secure, easy, and instant sale and download of product activation codes that are delivered and activated in real-time when the sale is made.

ESD benefits

For partners, you no longer need to stock Full Packaged Product which eliminates warehouse and shipping costs.

For your customers, this means fast access to Business Standard with immediate download and avoids the risk of lost or damaged products during shipping.
